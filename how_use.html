How To Use Result Sheet Generator

Follow these simple steps to create and print your result sheet.

Step 1: Enter Institution Details

Select institution type, enter name, address and ESTD year.

Step 2: Enter Student Information

Fill student name, roll number, department and class/semester.

Step 3: Add Subjects

Click "Add Subject" button to add subjects and marks fields.

Step 4: Enter Marks

Enter full marks and obtained marks for each subject.

Step 5: Generate Result

Click "Generate Sheet" to calculate total and result status.

Step 6: Print Result

Click "Print Result" button to print A4 format result sheet.
